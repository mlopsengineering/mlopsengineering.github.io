About

The idea for this project is simple: Provide a way to keep track of the latest advancements in artificial intelligence while by monitoring 1000s of data sources and keeping only high quality articles, blogs, papers and other feeds. Our newsletter is powered by a Jupyter notebook that runs in the cloud where we periodically update feeds and when a major advance occurs (example a new class of self-improving transformer model is discovered), we will update the "time to singularity" prediction in our newsletter with an explanation on why it was updated.
